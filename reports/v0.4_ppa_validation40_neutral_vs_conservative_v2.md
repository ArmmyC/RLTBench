## Recommendation

Recommendation: **promote to full public RTL-OPT validation**.

`conservative_area_v2` improved validity from 25/40 to 30/40 and improved the all-sample mean from 0.727439 to 0.843917. On paired-valid tasks, it improved 2, matched 21, and regressed 2. That is enough to justify the next full public RTL-OPT validation, while keeping the paired regressions visible for follow-up.

## Caveats

- `generic_cells` is a public-flow area proxy, not final silicon area.
- Power metrics are unavailable in this validation.
- Timing is not being optimized or claimed.
- This is public RTL-OPT validation only, not a SiliconCraft private benchmark.
- These results do not claim measured PPA improvement.
